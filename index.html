Who We Are

JustAI is a collective of developers, researchers, and ethical technologists dedicated to keeping artificial intelligence open, transparent, and community-driven. We believe the future of AI belongs to everyone — not just Big Tech.

Our Mission

Our goal is to accelerate innovation through open-source tools, models, and platforms. We're building AI that respects privacy, empowers individuals, and resists monopolization.

What We Do

  • Develop open-source large language models (LLMs)
  • Create privacy-first AI tools for real-world use
  • Support grassroots AI research and community projects
  • Advocate for digital sovereignty and open collaboration
